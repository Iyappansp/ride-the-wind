About Ride The Wind

Passionate about wind sports since 2009

Our Mission

At Ride The Wind, we believe everyone should experience the thrill of wind-powered water sports. Our mission is to provide world-class instruction in a safe, fun, and supportive environment.

Since 2009, we've trained over 2,500 students from complete beginners to competitive athletes. Our IKO-certified instructors bring decades of combined experience and a passion for teaching.

Safety is our top priority. All lessons include comprehensive safety briefings, modern equipment, and small class sizes to ensure personalized attention.
