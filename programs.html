Our Programs

From first-timer to freestyle pro - we have the perfect program for you

Beginner

Beginner Programs

Never touched a kite or windsurfer? Perfect! Our beginner programs start with the absolute basics and get you riding independently.

What You'll Learn:

  • Wind theory and weather reading
  • Equipment setup and safety systems
  • Kite/sail control on land and water
  • Body dragging and water starts
  • First successful rides
  • Self-rescue techniques
Book Beginner Lesson
Intermediate

Intermediate Programs

Already riding? Time to level up! Master upwind riding, smooth transitions, and prepare for your first jumps.

What You'll Master:

  • Consistent upwind riding
  • Smooth tack and jibe transitions
  • Board control and edge work
  • Power management in varying winds
  • Introduction to jumping
  • Video analysis and feedback
Book Intermediate Lesson
Advanced

Advanced Programs

Ready to push your limits? Our advanced coaching helps you master tricks, jumps, and competition-level skills.

Advanced Skills:

  • High jumps and controlled landings
  • Rotations: backrolls, frontrolls
  • Freestyle tricks and grabs
  • Wave riding techniques
  • Unhooked maneuvers
  • Competition preparation
